Project Experience

A short selection of recent organizational and consulting projects

Health & Development in Guatemala

As an Emory student, I managed the delivery of five distinct learning activities to over 700 Guatemalans of the Sololá region and the distribution of 1500 public health informational booklets over the course of the ten-day medical mission in March of 2020. In response to the COVID pandemic the following year, I continued our group's impact by leading the digital transformation of the program above to WhatsApp chatbot modules to expand healthcare education access to Guatemalan communities.

TAMID Consulting

Starting in 2018, I spent four years advising start-ups based in Israel's hi-tech ecosystem through pro-bono consulting. My last project involved strategizing and implementing business development efforts for a digital health start-up, and I also had led a four-month market research project for a social impact NGO that enabled the client to more effectively target partnerships with relevant US organizations.


Research at Rollins School of Public Health

Alongside my coursework at Emory, I was a Research Assistant for Dr. Puneet Chehal’s lab within the Health Policy & Management (HPM) department at Rollins. My role involved writing and reviewing code using national healthcare cost and utilization data for a collaborative project with Merck to explore healthcare disparities among patients with diabetes.


CuraLife

Three summers ago, I worked at a nutraceutical company focused on developing natural solutions for chronic disease management, where I supported the company's business development efforts in the US. My projects involved working across business units to develop education-based content tailored to healthcare practitioners for partnership-building purposes.
